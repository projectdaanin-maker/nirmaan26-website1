Who We Are

Building communities through the act of giving

Project Daan is a grassroots social impact initiative rooted in the spirit of daan — the act of giving. We mobilize residents, youth, and community leaders across residential societies to come together for meaningful social causes.


We believe change begins at home — in our neighbourhoods, our societies, and our everyday choices to give back.

What We Do

Our Core Initiatives

❀
Community Drives
Organizing collection drives for clothes, books, food, and essentials across residential societies to support those in need.
✿
Youth Volunteering
Engaging young residents and students in hands-on social work, building a generation rooted in empathy and responsibility.
❁
Awareness Campaigns
Running neighbourhood awareness programs on education, health, and sustainability to spark lasting community conversations.
✾
Fundraising for Causes
Channelling collective fundraising efforts toward verified NGOs and underprivileged communities with transparent impact reporting.
❃
Skill Workshops
Hosting free skill-building workshops for underprivileged youth — vocational training, spoken English, digital literacy, and more.
✽
Elder Support
Reaching out to elderly residents through companionship programs, health check-up camps, and community care initiatives.

Flagship Drive

Our Biggest Drive Yet

✦ May 2026 ✦
Nirmaan'26

Nirmaan'26 is our flagship community drive — scheduled for the first week of May 2026 across multiple residential societies. Through fundraising, volunteering, and awareness activities, we aim to demonstrate that small acts of many can create extraordinary change.
